Mini Zoo Animals
This page is intended as a guide for interns.

  • Bears

    • Bears are large heavy mammals of America and Eurasia that have long shaggy hair,
    rudimentary tails, and plantigrade feet and feed largely on fruit, plant matter, and insects as well as on flesh.

    The names of the Mini Zoo Bears are:

    1. Ollie
    2. Mona
    Additional Information on Bears

  • Giraffes

    • A giraffe is a large African mammal with a very long neck and forelegs, having a coat patterned with
    brown patches separated by lighter lines. It is the tallest living animal.

    The names of the Mini Zoo Giraffes are

    1. Frankie
    2. Coconut
    Additional Information on Giraffes

  • Lions

    • Lions are large tawny-colored cats that lives in prides, found in Africa and northwestern India.
    The male has a flowing shaggy mane and takes little part in hunting, which is done cooperatively by the females.

    The names of the Mini Zoo Lions are

    1. Mella
    2. Karl
    Additional Information on Lions

  • Monkeys

    • A Monkey is a small to medium-sized primate that typically has a long tail.
    Most monkeys live in trees in tropical countries.

    The names of the Mini Zoo Monkeys are

    1. Cookie
    2. Earl
    3. Banana Pudding
    Additional Information on Monkeys

  • Alligators

    • An Alligators is a large semi-aquatic reptile similar to a crocodile but with a broader and shorter head.
    Alligators are native to the Americas and China.

    The names of the Mini Zoo Alligators are

    1. Wren
    2. Aspen
    3. Mika

    Additional Information on Alligators
